Straight talk

Most IT companies make money by making things complicated. More complexity means more billable hours, more licenses to manage, more reasons you need them.

We do the opposite. We build systems that are as simple as they can be and only as complex as they need to be. We'll recommend the cheaper option when it's the right one. We'll tell you when something isn't worth fixing. We won't push a $40,000 server on a business that needs a $400 NAS.

We bill at a flat hourly rate, explain what we're doing in plain English, and we've been serving St. Louis businesses since the early 2000s. That's the whole pitch.
